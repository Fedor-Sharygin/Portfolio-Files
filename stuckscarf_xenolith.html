XENOLITH

UE5.3.2 Blueprint

XENOLITH is a horror game submitted to the Figerox Underwater Horror Game Jam. Main gameplay loop consists of walking through and taking notable photos of a set of underwater temples while deep monsters try to kill you. The enemy behaviors, checkpoint system, and photo taking mechanic were all made by me through the Unreal Engine's blueprint system.

The game was made in one week with a team of talented artists, composers, and level designers, which allowed me to focus on the game's mechanics instead of attempting to do everything by myself. It made me a more professional programmer and improved my experience as a team member.
