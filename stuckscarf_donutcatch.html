Donut Catch

Unity

This was my first personal game project, inspired by old handheld games such as 'Nu Pogodi', in which I used all of my knowledge of programming and couple years of studying game development at DigiPen. With this project I delved into Unity development process, widened my understanding of it and of the C# language. I have used my knowledge of Mathematical concepts such as Bezier Curves, to improve the process of editing the paths of the cupcakes.
